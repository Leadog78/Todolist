PERFECTSEASON

Draft five legends. Build chemistry. Chase the impossible 82–0.

How to play

  1. Spin the franchise. Each round the wheel deals you a team. Pick one of its legends.
  2. Fill all five spots — PG, SG, SF, PF, C. Play someone out of position and they lose effectiveness.
  3. Build chemistry. You need spacing, a playmaker, and a rim protector. Stacking ball-dominant scorers backfires.
  4. Use your tokens. Reroll deals a new team; the one-time Wildcard lets you draft any legend.
  5. Simulate 82 games. Watch it play out and see your record, grade, MVP, and biggest weakness.

Goal: construct the rare roster that runs the table at 82–0.
